3. **Image & Art** - Midjourney, DALL-E, Flux, Ideogram, etc.
4. **Video & Animation** - Kling, Runway, Pika, Opus.pro, etc.

7. **Design & Creative** - Canva AI, Figma AI, unicorn.studio, etc.
8. **Productivity & Automation** - Zapier, Make, relevanceai, etc.
9. **Research & Knowledge** - NotebookLM, Perplexity, Elicit, etc.
